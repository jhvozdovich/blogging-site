Denver 2020

The ESL Pro League will be coming to Denver for the finals of Season 11.

There are 24 teams competing and first place will win $750,000.

Rio 2020

Brazil has been home to some powerful teams such as MiBR, and now they will be hosting the next Major.

24 teams will be competing for a $1,000,000 prize at Jeunesse Arena in Rio from May21-24.

Cologne 2020

One of the largest ESL Pro Tour events, this will be the finale after a year of work for the competing teams.

Germany will be hosting 16 teams competing for a $1,000,000 in prize money.
